🧠 Informedi on Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

🏠 Early Life

Born: 15 October 1931, Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu

Dr. Kalam came from a poor but loving family. His father was a boat owner, and young Kalam sold newspapers to support the family. Despite financial struggles, he focused deeply on studies and discipline.

🎓 Education

He studied physics and aerospace engineering.

Kalam graduated from the Madras Institute of Technology and joined DRDO (Defence Research and Development Organisation), later moving to ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation).

🚀 Role in Indian Science

Known as: “Missile Man of India”

He played a key role in India’s missile programs like Agni and Prithvi. He was also involved in India’s 1998 nuclear tests at Pokhran, making India a nuclear power.

🇮🇳 President of India

Term: 2002 - 2007

Dr. Kalam became the 11th President of India and was known as the “People’s President”. He stayed simple and connected with the youth even while holding the highest office.

📖 Books Written

  • Wings of Fire (Autobiography)
  • Ignited Minds
  • India 2020

These books encourage young minds to dream big and work hard to make India a developed nation.

👨‍🏫 Passion for Teaching

Even after his presidency, he travelled across India giving lectures to students.

He believed:

“Dream is not what you see in sleep. Dream is something which doesn’t let you sleep.”

🕊️ Simple Life, Great Values

He lived a very simple life with honesty and discipline. He never owned expensive items or properties. He always followed the path of truth, peace, and dedication.
